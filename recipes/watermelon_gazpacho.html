Lasagna

Lasagna is a pasta dish made by layering wide, flat pasta with sauce, cheese, and optionally a meat or other protein.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed Wwatermelon
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 English cucumber, cubed
  • 2 plum tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons minced shallot
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  1. Combine fruit and vegetables alongside vinegar, lime juice, salt, and pepper in a high powered blender. Blend 30 seconds.
  2. Refrigerate 1 hour
  3. Stir before serving
