Lasagna

Lasagna is a pasta dish made by layering wide, flat pasta with sauce, cheese, and optionally a meat or other protein.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  1. Boil pasta and cool
  2. Cook ground beef
  3. Layer pasta, sauce, cheese, and ground beef
  4. Bake at 350 F for one hour
